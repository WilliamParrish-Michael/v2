About Ernesto

EP

Welcome! I'm Ernesto Paz, a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with over six years of clinical experience serving individuals, couples, and families throughout California. My approach to therapy is centered on holistic well-being and happiness rather than simply managing symptoms.

I believe that therapy should focus on happiness and each person's unique recipe for that. My goal is to partner with you in creating a life filled with fulfillment, joy, and peace of mind. Together, we'll work collaboratively to develop solutions and build your confidence in achieving your personal goals.

As a Certified Mindfulness-Informed Clinician and DBT Skills Training Certified therapist, I integrate evidence-based practices with a warm, authentic approach. I create a safe, non-judgmental space that respects all backgrounds and encourages authentic exploration and growth.

When I'm not in session, I enjoy an active lifestyle with my wife and three sons - mountain biking, snowboarding, surfing, and exploring the outdoors. This balance in my own life reflects the holistic approach I bring to therapy.

My Therapeutic Approach

🌟 Happiness-Focused

Unlike traditional symptom-reduction models, I focus on helping you experience higher states of fulfillment, joy, and peace of mind.

🤝 Collaborative Partnership

You're the expert on your own life. I work with you as a partner to develop solutions and build confidence in achieving your goals.

🎯 Solution-Focused

My action-oriented, mindful approach emphasizes practical solutions and real-world applications of therapeutic insights.

🌍 Culturally Responsive

I respect all backgrounds and create an inclusive space where everyone feels understood and valued for who they are.
