About Ernesto

A collaborative approach to mental health focused on fulfillment, not just fixing problems

EP

Welcome! I'm Ernesto Paz, a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with over six years of clinical experience serving individuals, couples, and families throughout California. My approach to therapy is fundamentally different from traditional models.

I believe that therapy should focus on happiness and each person's unique recipe for that. Rather than simply managing symptoms, my goal is to partner with you in creating a life filled with fulfillment, joy, and peace of mind.

As a Certified Mindfulness-Informed Clinician and DBT Skills Training Certified therapist, I integrate evidence-based practices with a warm, collaborative approach. My extensive background in addiction treatment has equipped me to work with incredibly diverse populations across all backgrounds.

Beyond my professional identity, I'm a dedicated family man, married with three sons. I maintain an active lifestyle—mountain biking, snowboarding, surfing, and exploring the outdoors—which reflects the holistic approach I bring to therapy.

My Therapeutic Philosophy

A holistic, collaborative approach that treats you as the expert on your own life

🌟

Happiness-Focused Approach

Unlike traditional symptom-reduction models, I focus on helping you experience higher states of fulfillment, joy, and peace of mind. Therapy should be about creating the life you want, not just fixing what's wrong.

🤝

Collaborative Partnership

You're the expert on your own life. I work with you as a partner to develop solutions and build confidence in achieving your personal goals. Together, we create your unique recipe for happiness.

🎯

Action-Oriented Solutions

My approach is practical and solution-focused. We don't just talk about problems—we develop real-world strategies and skills you can use immediately to create positive change in your life.

🌍

Culturally Responsive Care

I respect all backgrounds and create an inclusive space where everyone feels understood and valued. My extensive experience with diverse populations ensures culturally competent care.
